About the Institute

Established in 2002, Vishwakarma Institute of Information Technology (VIIT) is a premier engineering college in Pune, Maharashtra. Run by the Bansilal Ramnath Agarwal Charitable Trust (BRACT), the institute is known for its academic rigor and vibrant campus life.

Autonomy

Granted autonomous status, allowing for a dynamic curriculum that evolves with industry standards.

Accreditation

Accredited with an 'A' Grade by NAAC and programs accredited by NBA. ISO 9001:2015 certified.

Location

Located in Kondhwa (Budruk), offering a serene learning environment distinct from the city center.

Academics

VIIT offers a wide range of Undergraduate (B.Tech), Postgraduate (M.Tech), and Doctoral (PhD) programs. The curriculum focuses on project-based learning and industry internships.

Admissions & Eligibility

Entrance Exams

Admissions are primarily based on scores from:

  • MHT-CET (Maharashtra State Candidates)
  • JEE Main (All India Candidates)
  • GATE (For M.Tech)

Eligibility Criteria

B.Tech: Passed 10+2 with Physics and Math as compulsory subjects, along with Chem/Bio/Tech subject. Min 45% marks (40% for reserved categories).

Application Mode

Centralized Admission Process (CAP) conducted by the State Common Entrance Test Cell, Maharashtra.

Placements
